About


As an employee of Job Resources, you are entitled to benefits after 10 weeks of working at Job Resources and are in good standing with the company. Good standing meaning that you are a reliable employee, such as showing up for your shifts on time and producing good quality work for that probation period of 10 weeks. After those 10 weeks, you are able to apply to the company provided insurance through this website.

Job Resources provided medical, eye, and dental insurance to their employees. The insurance provider for medical is Blue Cross Blue Sheild Minnesota and Davis Vision for eye and dental insurance.

An employee can apply by entering their information in the form below. Make sure to insert all the right infomation as errors could cause issue, like extended period of waiting for approval or dental or just straight up denial.

The way that an employee can tell if they have been approved or denied is via the label above. It will either say, not apply, for employees that have not applied yet or are not able to apply yet, approved, for employees that have submitted the application and the insurance company as approved it, or denied, for employees that have been denied coverage by the insurance company. Any questions about insurance or denial is to be directed to the insurance company directly.
