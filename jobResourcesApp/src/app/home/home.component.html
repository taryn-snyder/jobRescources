About

This is a website for employees of Job Resources. Job Resources is a company deticated to providing the best experience to both its employees and its customers. This website provides many resource for our employees. An employee can check many sources they might need while working at this company.

The Time Card tab allows employees to check the schedule of up to the next 3 weeks.

The Benefits tab allows employees to check information about benefits provided by Job Resources. It allows employees to submit an application to be reviewed by the insurance companies. It also allows employees to check whether that application has been approved, denied, or that the employee has not applied yet.

Human Resources allows employees to view information about Human Resources and submit a form of complaint about any applicable grieviances an employee many have with another employee at Job Resources or any policies at Job Resources.

The Account tab allows an employees to check their account information.

The Login tab allows employees to login to view information specific to them in the above tabs.
